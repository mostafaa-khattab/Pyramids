The pyramids are tombs for ancient Egyptian pharaohs and their kings and queens.

Pharaohs were mummified and buried inside pyramids with many treasures to ensure that the pharaoh

had a good life in the afterlife.

The pyramids wstyle are also built to keep thieves away from the gold and offerings in which the pharaohs were buried.

The pyramids are built of stone and have many hidden rooms inside.

The Egyptian pyramids are one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

It was built by the ancient Egyptians between 2630 and 1530 BC.

They were also used as tombs for kings

The Pyramid of Khufu, located west of Giza, is the largest among them.
